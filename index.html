System involving video cameras and signals to batters revealed!

In a stunning revelation, former Houston Astros pitcher Mike Friers, now a member of the Oakland Athletics, shared with journalists the damning accusation that the 2017 World Series Champion Houston Astros cheated their way to the franchise's first title. MLB fans around the world are in an uproar for the token measures taken to punish those responsible for creating the greatest stain on the integrity of the game since the Black Sox scandal of 1919. One thing is for sure, the 2020 season is going to be a long, hard walk of shame for Astros players when they play on the road as fans from around the league won't be so forgiving as Mr. Manfred.
